About the Studio

The Story Behind the Lens

🎬

Lumis Studio was founded in 2013 by Marcus Veil — a cinematographer with a background in documentary film and electronic music composition. What began as a one-camera operation in a converted warehouse has grown into a full-service media production house serving brands, musicians, and storytellers worldwide.

We believe that every frame, every note, and every photograph is an opportunity to communicate something that words alone cannot. Our work spans commercial advertising, independent documentary, editorial photography, and original music — all unified by a commitment to intentional craft.

How we work together

🧭

Discovery Sprint

Every project begins with a shared discovery session where the full team aligns on goals, references, and creative direction. No silos, no briefings-by-proxy — everyone hears the vision firsthand.

🛠️

Cross-Discipline Production

Our photographers brief the editors. Our composers sit in on colour grading. This cross-pollination is deliberate — the best creative decisions happen at the intersection of disciplines.

🔄

Iterative Review

Two structured review cycles are built into every engagement. We present work-in-progress, gather focused feedback, and refine — no endless revision loops, no last-minute surprises.

🚀

Delivery & Handoff

Final files are delivered with full technical documentation, format variants, and a 30-day post-delivery support window. We stay available until you're confident with what you have.

Studio Culture

We run a flat structure. There are no account managers between you and the people creating your work. The person you brief on day one is the person who delivers on the last day.

We take on a limited number of projects each quarter — intentionally. It means every client gets the full attention of the team, not the leftovers.
